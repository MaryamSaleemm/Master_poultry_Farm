About Masters Poultry

Since 1995.

Our Story

Located in Punjab in Chichawatni (Chak 176/9L), Masters Poultry Farm began with a simple mission: to provide the freshest, highest-quality table eggs to wholesalers across the region.

What started as a small flock has grown into a modern, semi-automated operation housing over 100,000 birds. We understand the market's need for consistency. That is why our infrastructure is designed to ensure a steady daily supply, regardless of the season.

We are not just selling eggs; we are building long-term partnerships with traders in Rawalpindi, Lahore, and KPK.
